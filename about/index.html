About me


My name is Xuanming “XM” Zhou. I’m a game designer, teacher, dad and all-round geek. I also really like bullet points, here are a few:

  • 15 years experience in game design, with 13 published titles.
  • Background in illustration and fine art.
  • Self-taught programmer fluent in C# and Java.
  • Built my own geek t-shirt label, Xmashed Gear.
  • Taught game design at DMAD and ADM.
