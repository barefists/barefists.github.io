Frogs in Love

March 7, 2008 Xuanming Games

Developed and published independently by Boomzap Entertainment, Frogs in Love is a mini-game collection distributed by prominent websites such as Big Fish Games and WildTangent. You embark on a quest to win over the heart of Jasmine, the most beautiful frog in the pond, so you can dance with her at the flower festival. Play games like River Jumping, Skeet Shooting, Treasure Hunt and more, gaining the trust and guidance of other critters in the pond for your quest.

As the Lead Game Designer within the team, my responsibilities include leading design discussions, level design, scripting gameplay for all mini-games, user interface wire-framing and design documentation.
