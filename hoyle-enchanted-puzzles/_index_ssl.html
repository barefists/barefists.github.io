Hoyle Enchanted Puzzles

July 3, 2008 Xuanming Games

Published by Encore on various platforms including Amazon and WildTangent, Hoyle Enchanted Puzzles puts the player in the shoes of a young apprentice, training to become a powerful wizard by playing a variety of puzzles.

The game features more than 200 levels over 9 games types, including Match-3, Racing, Fishing, Solitaire, Tile Inlay, and Mahjong.

My responsibilities within the team was to build levels, script gameplay in the Lua language and balance the game using Microsoft Excel.
