Awakening: Moonfell Wood

Big Fish Games (2010), PC/Mac/iOS/Android
Testing

Play as Sophia, the young princess who escaped the dreamless castle, and explore Moonfell Wood to find the rest of humankind. The woods are full of strange spirits, perplexing puzzles, and a mysterious witch! With no magic of your own, you’ve only got your wits and cleverness to help you survive. Can you find the other humans and save Moonfell Wood from the darkness? Get the game on Steam or at Big Fish Games.

Assisted lead game designer by doing internal testing, bug reports and confirming fixes.
