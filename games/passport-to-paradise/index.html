Passport to Paradise

iWin (2009), PC
Additional Design, Testing

Published by iWin in 2009 on their own site and Big Fish Games, Passport to Paradise is a time-management sim that challenges the player to build the ultimate luxury resort on a beautiful remote island.

You play Kate Summers – a busy real estate executive from New York. Kate is on vacation from the daily grind, and the beach resort she is visiting turns out to be a total mess! Your job is to help Kate turn a failing resort into a tropical paradise. Along the way you will build accommodations and sightseeing attractions, manage the resort’s cash flow, and upgrade natural attractions such as hot springs and a volcano. Harness your real estate skills to help her turn the island of Pulau Kelapa and 4 other tropical islands into world-class beach resorts in Passport to Paradise!

I helped with scripting, balancing and testing with this title.
