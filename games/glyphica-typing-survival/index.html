Glyphica: Typing Survival

Independent (2024), PC/Mac/Linux
Sole Developer (C & Javascript)

Glyphica is a typing-roguelike that blends survival and strategy. Type to shoot. Test your keyboard mastery, unlock powerful upgrades, and craft overpowered builds to destroy hordes of enemies. How fast can you type?

Development for Glyphica began in my free time, exploring the development potential of the open-source game engine, neoSphere. While experimenting with the engine, I put together a prototype that proved to be popular with colleagues and eventually developed the prototype into a full game in 2024. Engine modifications written in C, and game scripted in Javascript (ES2016). Minimal sprites were used for the game, with the majority of visuals created using primitives.
