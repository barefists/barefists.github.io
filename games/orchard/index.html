Orchard

Majesco Entertainment (2009), PC
Additional Design, Testing

Published by Majesco Entertainment in 2009 on Big Fish Games, Orchard is a time-management sim that let’s players manage a struggling farm and bring it back into profitability.

Out of the blue, you receive a letter from your granduncle Fred, offering you the change you’ve been yearning for. He’s had enough of running the family orchard and is bequeathing it to you. It’s time to head down to the country and get your hands dirty, making juices, pies, jellies, and more in Orchard!

I worked on character design, environment design and testing on this title.
